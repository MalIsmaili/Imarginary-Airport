Passenger Rules

Important guidelines to ensure a smooth and safe travel experience.

Passenger Rules and Guidelines

Baggage Rules

  • Carry-on luggage must not exceed the airline's weight and size restrictions.
  • Checked luggage should be securely packed and within the allowable weight limit.
  • Items such as sharp objects, flammable substances, and explosives are strictly prohibited.

Goods Allowed/Prohibited

  • Liquids, gels, and aerosols in carry-on bags must be in containers of 100ml or less.
  • Food and beverages are allowed but may be subject to additional screening.
  • Prohibited goods include firearms, illegal drugs, and hazardous materials.

Lost Baggage

If your baggage is lost, report it immediately to the airline's customer service desk. Provide a detailed description of your bag and its contents. Compensation will be handled according to the airline's policies.

Behavior and Conduct

  • All passengers are required to comply with security and safety protocols.
  • Disruptive behavior or refusal to follow instructions may result in denied boarding.
  • Smoking is only allowed in designated areas.
