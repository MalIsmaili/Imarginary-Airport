Parking & Car Rental

Convenient parking options and trusted car rental services at Adem Jashari International Airport.

Parking Options

Short-Term Parking

Located near the terminal, our short-term parking is ideal for dropping off or picking up passengers. Rates start at €1 per hour.

Long-Term Parking

Secure and affordable, long-term parking is perfect for travelers leaving their vehicles for extended periods. Rates start at €5 per day.

Payment Methods

We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payments at all parking facilities. Automated kiosks are available for convenience.

Car Rental Services

Available Rental Companies

  • Hertz
  • Europcar
  • Sixt
  • Enterprise

How to Rent a Car

Visit the car rental desks located in the arrivals hall. Be sure to have your driver’s license, passport, and a credit card for payment.

Contact Information

For more details, contact the car rental service desk directly. Assistance is available 24/7 to help with your travel needs.
