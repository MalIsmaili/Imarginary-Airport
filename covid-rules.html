COVID-19 Airline Rules

Your safety is our priority. Follow these updated guidelines for a safe journey.

Vaccination Requirements

Many airlines require proof of vaccination for international travel. Check your airline's policy and ensure your documentation is up to date.

Testing Requirements

A negative COVID-19 test is mandatory for certain destinations. Testing should be done within 48 hours before departure.

Mask Mandates

Masks are required in terminals and during flights for all passengers over 2 years old. Ensure your mask meets safety standards.

Hygiene Protocols

Sanitizing stations are available throughout the airport. Passengers are encouraged to maintain social distancing and practice good hygiene.
